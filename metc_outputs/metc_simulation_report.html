METC amendment CRM simulation report

Each simulated trial is initialized with six fully followed patients at L1 (5x5 Gy), zero acute toxicity and zero subacute toxicity. The first new cohort of three patients starts at L2 (5x6 Gy). Maximum sample size is 30 including the six existing patients.

Design settings

  • Simulations per combination: 200
  • Scenario grid: 5 acute toxicity scenarios × 3 EWOC modes × 2 burn-in settings = 30 combinations.
  • Acute target: 0.20; subacute target: 0.33; EWOC alpha: 0.35.
  • Prior skeleton acute: 0.021, 0.066, 0.150, 0.266, 0.396; subacute: 0.036, 0.084, 0.157, 0.250, 0.355.
  • Trial duration is reported as one patient per four weeks: additional duration = new patients × 4 weeks.
  • True MTD is defined as the highest dose level with true acute toxicity ≤ target acute toxicity.
